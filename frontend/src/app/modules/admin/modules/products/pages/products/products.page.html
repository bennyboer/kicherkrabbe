Products

A product will consist of the following:

  • Product number (equal to the article number)
  • Links to the used pattern and fabrics (can be multiple)
  • The composition of the used fabrics (cotton, elasthan, polyester, ...)
  • A text (text area that supports formatting) of the used fabrics (Charge-ID and producer of the fabrics) and additional things like buttons, zippers, ...
  • A text (text area that supports formatting) of risks ("Sicherheitshinweise")
  • A date of when the product was produced (producedAt)
  • A date of when the product was created (createdAt)
